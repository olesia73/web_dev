Kvas

Slavic bread drink: like non-alcoholic beer, but not lame

For this lovely beverage, you'll need:

  • 2.5 gallons of water
    • If you're one for metric units, sowwy
  • 1 lb or 9 slices of rye bread
  • 1 handful of raisins
  • 1.8 lb (4 cups) of sugar
  • 1.5 Tbsp of active dry yeast
  • 3 large plastic soda bottles

And now... the recipe

  1. Boil the water in a large pot
  2. In the meantime, toast the bread
    3. Darkness of toast begets darkness of kvas
  3. Remove the water pot from the heat
  4. Add a handful of raisins and toasted bread to the pot
  5. Cover it with the lid and let it stay overnight
  6. Remove and discard the bread
  7. Add yeast and sugar to the pot
  8. Stirrrrrrrr
  9. Cover with the lid and leave on the counter for 6 hours
  10. Don't forget to stir every hour or so
  11. Discard the raisins
  12. Pour into plastic bottles and leave in the fridge overnight
    13. Ta-da! Your weird fermented brew is ready.
